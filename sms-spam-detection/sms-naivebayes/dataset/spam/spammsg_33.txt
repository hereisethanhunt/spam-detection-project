Swayamvara By David Hair,‘Swayamvara’ (The Return of Ravana Book 2) by David Hair is a story of reincarnation, two swayamvaras, 800 years apart, that sees four lives knotted forever & one rigid force, Ravana reborn. 
In Return of Ravana series, the action oscillates from the floodlit studios of Mumbai to the glittering Rajput courts, while Vikram-Chand & his friends battle an age-old rival whose power is growing like never before.
